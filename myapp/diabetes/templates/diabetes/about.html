Diabetes, specifically type 2 diabetes, is an endocrinological disorder that often presents without symptoms or nonspecific symptoms. Patients may present late, and therefore are at risk of developing complications of different body systems, such as eyes (diabetic retinopathy), kidneys (diabetic nephropathy), or peripheral nerves (mononeuritis multiplex). Early diagnosis and intervention is key in reducing complications and therefore improving patient prognosis. The DSS will utilize advanced data mining methods to predict patient risk of diabetes.

Early-Stage Diabetes Diagnosis Functions:

  • Users: nurses, pysicians, and public healthcare provider
  • Nurses can take vitals of the patients and save the data into MySQL database. The application will train the model again after the new datapoint added in.
  • Physician can input the patients data and the application will report the likelihood of a potential diabetes, and give treatment suggestions to risky patients.
  • Application illustrates the statistical information with the latest dataset.
